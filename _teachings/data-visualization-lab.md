## Course Overview

A lab-oriented course on designing and building effective data visualizations. Covered visualization theory, perceptual principles, interactive graphics, and practical tooling for transforming complex datasets into clear, communicative visual narratives.

Taught by **Dr. Francisco Rubio** at NYU Tandon, Spring 2023.
