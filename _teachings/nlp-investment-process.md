## Course Overview

Explored the application of natural language processing techniques to the investment process. Covered text mining of financial documents (SEC filings, earnings calls, news), sentiment analysis for trading signals, and information extraction from unstructured financial data.

Taught by **Dr. Daniel Rodriguez** at NYU Tandon, Fall 2022.
